To my beloved,

Love,

Your Secret Admirer



My technical collaborator was Toby Kwan. I made my generator to be a short poetic love letter generator from a secret admirer. I got my text from manybooks.net from some love letters in A Woman's Love Letters by Sophie M. Almon-Hensley. I was inspired by browsing through the romance section of the site. One line has 100-140 chars. I wanted full words so I used word n-grams with a length of 4. I styled the background to be pink with a lace design because I wanted to make it girly and stand out since it is supposed to be an anonyomous letter, so the person who receives it should not be able to miss it. I chose to have a font similar to a handwritten style because it emulates a handwritten note. I kept the opening and ending of the love letter to have a default because I wanted only the body of the letter to be randomly generated.
